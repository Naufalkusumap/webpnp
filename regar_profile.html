Herianto Siregar

S.H., M.H., CRA., CLI.
Senior Partner

With over 15 years of distinguished legal practice, Siregar specializes in banking litigation and has represented major financial institutions and state-owned enterprises throughout Indonesia.

Professional Profile

Herianto Siregar brings more than 15 years of high-stakes legal experience to his role as Senior Partner at Perdana Putra & Partners. A specialist in banking litigation, Siregar has established himself as one of Indonesia's foremost legal experts in financial sector disputes and bankruptcy proceedings.

His practice combines deep technical knowledge with strategic insight, having represented major Indonesian banks including Bank Bukopin, Bank Tabungan Negara (BTN), and Bank Rakyat Indonesia (BRI) in complex litigation matters. Siregar's reputation for excellence extends beyond the private sector, having served as personal advocate to Akbar Tandjung, former Chairman of Indonesia's People's Representative Council (DPR RI) and minister during the New Order era.

In the realm of bankruptcy law, Siregar has successfully acted as Asset Manager in high-profile cases including Semen Bosowa Maros (Makassar Commercial Court, 2021) and Harapan Jaya Mandiri Perkasa (Central Jakarta Commercial Court), demonstrating his ability to navigate complex restructuring scenarios.

Banking Litigation Expertise
