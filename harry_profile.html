Harry Rizki Perdana Putra

S.T., S.H., M.H., CRA
Managing Partner

With over 10 years of legal practice, Harry specializes in commercial litigation, bankruptcy, and corporate law, representing major cases in Indonesia's legal landscape.

Professional Profile

A former activist turned legal practitioner with more than a decade of experience, Harry Rizki Perdana Putra has established himself as a formidable force in Indonesian legal circles. His extensive network and courtroom expertise have positioned him as a go-to attorney for complex corporate disputes, high-stakes corruption cases, and intricate bankruptcy proceedings.

Harry's practice spans multiple sectors including banking, palm oil industry, and digital commerce. As a licensed Curator Manager, he has successfully overseen numerous Suspension of Debt Payment and Bankruptcy cases, managing assets valued at approximately USD 50 million. His strategic approach to debt restructuring and asset management has earned him recognition in Indonesia's legal community.

Beyond traditional legal practice, Harry has served as a Digital Ecosystem Consultant, bridging the gap between technology and legal frameworks. His forward-thinking approach to digital commerce regulation has made him a sought-after advisor in Indonesia's rapidly evolving tech landscape.

Notable Cases & Achievements

  • Represented Indonesia's largest marketplace platform in landmark antitrust proceedings before the Business Competition Supervisory Commission (KPPU)
  • Defended clients in high-profile corruption cases at both the Corruption Eradication Commission (KPK) and the Attorney General's Office involving state-owned enterprises and private entities
  • Served as Curator Manager for bankruptcy estates with combined assets exceeding USD 50 million
  • Provided strategic counsel as Digital Ecosystem Consultant for major e-commerce platforms
  • Former Audit Committee member on the Board of Commissioners of PT Perkebunan Nusantara V (now Palm Co)
